Try out these local resaurants!

Lakeside Charlie's

A great place to try if you are looking for a view of the water. Right across from Lake Mitchell (and I waitressed there for a summer so I can vouch for the food)

Herraduras

A great Mexican restaurant with a fun atmosphere. Lots of options and huge portions - I recommend the fajitas!

Clam Lake

An interesting place for those into craft beers, as this spot is also a brewery! In the middle of downtown so an eady place to stop in.
