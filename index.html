A little bit about Cadillac...

I grew up in Cadillac, and I love coming back to visit! Although it may appear as any other small town, there's actually lots of fun stuff to do and see!

Cadillac is a great city for those who love water activities - growing up, I spent countless hours playing in Cadillac's 2 lakes (Lake Mitchell and Lake Cadillac)

I think it is a great harmony of both small town community and getting to enjoy the outdoors while not being too far from bigger cities like Traverse City and Grand Rapids.

University of Michigan School of Information Isabella Valice 2020
